Why calm & early sheltering matters

Many exposures are avoidable if people shelter indoors, close ventilation, and avoid contaminated food/water. This module shows exactly what to do.

Quick Quiz — Nuclear Safety

Quick actions

  • Go inside and close all windows/doors immediately
  • Turn off fans, HVAC and seal gaps
  • Do not eat uncovered food; use sealed water
  • Only evacuate when official order is given
