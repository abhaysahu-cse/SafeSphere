🌍 Earthquake Safety Basics

Learn essential preparedness, safety rules, and real-life response techniques for earthquakes.

1. What is an Earthquake?

Earthquakes happen when plates below Earth’s surface move. Energy builds up due to friction, and when released suddenly, the ground shakes.

  • 🌋 Caused by movement of tectonic plates
  • ⚡ Sudden release of pressure
  • 🌍 No one can predict exact time

2. What You May Feel During Earthquake

  • Light shaking of the floor
  • Furniture & windows vibrating
  • Fans & hanging objects swinging
  • Strong quakes: difficult to walk or stand

3. The Most Important Rule: DROP – COVER – HOLD ON

  • ⬇ DROP to your knees – prevents falling
  • 🛡 COVER head & neck under table or using arms
  • ✊ HOLD ON until shaking stops

Never do this:

  • ❌ Do NOT run outside
  • ❌ Stay away from windows & glass
  • ❌ Do not stand near cupboards or fans
  • ❌ Never use elevators

4. What to Do in Different Places

If inside a building:

  • Drop, Cover, Hold On
  • Stay away from windows & lights
  • Stay inside until shaking stops

If in bed:

  • Stay in bed & cover your head with a pillow

If outside:

  • Go to open ground
  • Away from buildings, trees & electric poles

If in a vehicle:

  • Stop vehicle safely, stay inside
  • Avoid bridges, flyovers, tunnels

5. After the Earthquake

  • Stay calm & evacuate safely
  • Follow school evacuation route
  • Do not run or push others
  • Watch for broken glass and wires
  • Go to assembly point

6. Earthquake Emergency Kit

  • 🧴 First Aid Kit
  • 📱 Emergency contact card
  • 🔦 Torch
  • 📢 Whistle
  • 🚰 Water bottle
  • 📦 Snacks
  • 🔋 Power bank
