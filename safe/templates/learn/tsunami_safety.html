1. Tsunami Science

Tsunamis are caused by undersea earthquakes, volcanic eruptions, or landslides. Unlike normal waves, tsunami waves involve the entire water column.

Key Fact: In deep ocean, tsunami waves are fast but low. Near shore, they slow down and grow dangerously tall.

2. Natural Warning Signs

  • Strong or long coastal earthquakes
  • Sea suddenly receding
  • Loud ocean roar (like a train)

3. Survival Rules

Golden Rule: After shaking, RUN to high ground immediately. Do not wait for alerts.

Never go to the shore to watch waves. Multiple waves may strike.

4. After the Tsunami

Flooding, debris, and contaminated water are deadly hazards.

Stay away until authorities declare the area safe.
