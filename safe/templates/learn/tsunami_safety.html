1. Tsunami Science

Tsunamis are generated mainly by undersea earthquakes (M > 7.0). Energy transfer displaces massive water volumes.

Tsunami speed can exceed 800 km/h in deep oceans.

2. Natural Warning Signs

  • Strong coastal earthquake
  • Sudden sea withdrawal
  • Unusual roaring sound

3. Early Warning Systems

India uses INCOIS Tsunami Warning Centre with seismic sensors, buoys and satellites.

Alerts are issued within 10 minutes of detection.

4. Evacuation Science

Evacuation time window is often less than 30 minutes after a nearby earthquake.

Vertical evacuation buildings may be used where hills are unavailable.

5. Structural Safety

  • Reinforced concrete buildings resist flow better
  • Wooden structures float and collapse easily
  • Upper floors are safer than ground floors
