1. What is Civil Defence?

Civil Defence is the organized effort to protect civilians, infrastructure, and essential services during war, attacks, or national emergencies.

Main Goal: Reduce casualties & maintain public order.

2. BEFORE War / Attack (Preparedness)

  • Identify nearest shelters
  • Learn siren signals
  • Store emergency supplies
  • Participate in mock drills

3. Air Raid Warning Signals

  • Long Wailing Siren: Incoming attack
  • Short Intermittent Siren: All clear

4. DURING Attack

Indoors: Basement, windowless rooms, lie flat & cover head.
  • Switch off lights (blackout)
  • Stay away from windows
  • Follow official announcements

5. Chemical / Nuclear Threat

  • Seal doors & windows
  • Cover nose with wet cloth
  • Do NOT consume exposed food/water

6. AFTER Attack

  • Check for injuries
  • Avoid damaged buildings
  • Report unexploded objects
  • Assist authorities calmly
