1. What is Civil Defence?

Civil Defence is the organized effort to protect civilians from military attacks, natural disasters, and internal disturbances during war or emergencies.

Key Objective: Minimize loss of life and maintain essential services.

2. Air Raid Warning Signals

  • Wailing Siren: Incoming attack warning
  • Short Bursts: All clear signal

3. During Bombing or Attack

Indoors: Move to basement, avoid windows, lie flat and cover head.

Outdoors: Take cover in trenches or behind solid structures.

4. Chemical / Nuclear Threat

  • Cover nose & mouth with wet cloth
  • Seal doors & windows
  • Listen to official instructions
