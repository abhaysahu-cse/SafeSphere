🏠 Safety Checklist

  • Unplug appliances
  • Stay indoors
  • Avoid metal objects
  • Secure loose items

1. What is a Thunderstorm?

A thunderstorm is a weather system characterized by lightning, thunder, heavy rain, gusty winds, and sometimes hail or tornadoes.

Fact: Lightning can strike up to 10 km away from rain.

2. Lightning Science (Why it Kills)

  • Direct strike
  • Side flash
  • Ground current
  • Conduction through metal

3. Outdoor Safety Rules

DO NOT: Stand under trees, hold metal, or stay in open fields.

If trapped outside: crouch low, feet together, minimize ground contact.

4. Indoor Safety Rules

  • Unplug electronics
  • Avoid plumbing & wired phones
  • Stay away from windows

5. Vehicles & Travel

Cars with metal roofs act as Faraday cages. Avoid motorcycles and open vehicles.

6. After the Storm

  • Watch for fallen power lines
  • Avoid flooded roads
  • Provide first aid if safe
