🚑 Emergency First Aid – Critical Life-Saving Techniques

✅ 1. Core Theory Students Must Learn

⭐ A. Check — Call — Care

  • CHECK surroundings – ensure safety
  • CALL 108 or emergency help
  • CARE and give first aid till help arrives

⭐ B. ABC of First Aid

  • A – Airway: Is the airway open?
  • B – Breathing: Is breathing normal?
  • C – Circulation: Check bleeding / heartbeat

⭐ C. First Aid Golden Rule

Do no further harm — Don’t move victim unless unsafe.

🚨 2. Life-Saving First Aid Techniques

🔵 A. CPR (Hands-Only)

  • Flat surface → Kneel → Interlock hands
  • Push hard/fast in chest center
  • Rate: 100–120 / min
  • Continue until help arrives

🔵 B. Severe Bleeding

  • Apply firm pressure with cloth
  • Raise injured part
  • Do not remove soaked cloth

🔵 C. Fractures

  • Don’t move injured part
  • Use splint (stick / cardboard)
  • Use sling & tie around limb

🔵 D. Burns

  • Cool water 10–20 min
  • Cover with clean cloth
  • No ghee/oil/paste/ice

🔵 E. Drowning

  • Lay on back → Check breathing
  • Start CPR if needed
  • Turn head sideways to drain water

🔵 F. Electric Shock

  • Switch off power first
  • Don’t touch directly
  • Use wood/dry cloth

🔵 G. Choking – Heimlich

  • Fist above navel → Upward thrusts
  • Children: 5 back blows + 5 chest thrusts

🔵 H. Snake Bite

  • Keep still
  • Tie bandage above bite
  • Don’t cut or suck
  • Hospital immediately

🎒 3. First Aid Kit Must Include

  • Bandages, gauze, antiseptic
  • Scissors, gloves
  • Flashlight, whistle
  • ORS, pain relievers
  • Emergency numbers card
