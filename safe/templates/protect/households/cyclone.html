Why cyclones cause property loss — the real causes

  • Roof uplift: wind lifts loose roof sheets if not anchored—entire roof can be lost.
  • Windows & openings: broken windows create internal pressure that pushes walls out.
  • Flying debris: can puncture roofs, break glass and damage water tanks/solar panels.
  • Poor drainage & ponding: traps water at foundations and causes structural weakening.
  • Improper storage of fuels/tools: leads to secondary fires or chemical contamination after floodwater.
Focus: strengthen roof anchorage, protect openings, secure loose items, and keep water away from the foundation.

FAQ

How much does retrofitting cost?
Minor retrofits (straps, bolts, shutters) are low-cost — often a few thousand rupees — far less than replacing a roof. Larger structural retrofits cost more but increase safety and insurance eligibility.

High-priority checklist (summary)

  • Anchor roofs with straps / bolts
  • Board or shutter windows
  • Elevate important items above flood level
  • Secure water tanks & solar panels
  • Know nearest cyclone shelter

Emergency kit

  • 3 days water & food
  • First-aid & medicines
  • Documents in waterproof folder
  • Battery radio / torch / powerbank

Important: documentation

Photograph structure, roof, and valuables now — these photos accelerate relief and insurance claims.
