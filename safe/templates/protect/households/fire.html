Why this module matters

Frequently asked

What type of extinguisher is best for household?
A multi-purpose ABC dry powder extinguisher covers most household fires; CO₂ can be used for small electrical fires. If unsure, evacuate and call the fire service.
Do I need a fire blanket?
Yes — a simple fire blanket is excellent for smothering pan / small fires and for wrapping a person whose clothes are on fire.

Quick actions

  • Install a smoke alarm on each floor
  • Keep a charged torch and phone near bed
  • Know at least two escape routes from sleeping rooms
  • Keep fire extinguisher or blanket in kitchen
