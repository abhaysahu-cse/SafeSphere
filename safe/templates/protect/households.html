🏠 Households & Property Owners

Practical, low-cost steps to protect your home, documents and belongings before, during and after disasters.

🌊

Floods — Elevate valuables & documents

Move important papers and medicines to higher shelves, unplug electricals, and have an emergency bag ready with drinking water.

Before · During · After
🌀

Cyclones — Roof & window protection

Secure roof sheets, board windows when possible, tie down loose items and follow official evacuation orders early.

Wind · Secure property
🔥

Heatwaves — Cooling & hydration

Keep water ready, avoid outdoor work during peak heat, and look out for the elderly and children. Use cool roofs or shading where possible.

Hydration · Cooling
🧯

House Fire — Prevention & escape

Keep flammables away from cooking, test smoke alarms, make a household escape plan and an outdoor meeting point.

Prevention · Escape plan
🌍

Earthquake — Drop, Cover & Hold

Practice drop-cover-hold drills, secure tall furniture, and keep an emergency bag with first-aid and water.

Drills · Securing home
