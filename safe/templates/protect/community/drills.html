Why community drills matter

Mock drills reduce panic, clarify roles, improve evacuation time and reveal logistical gaps — especially for elders, disabled people and animals.

  • Familiarises everyone with routes and assembly points
  • Trains volunteers to manage crowds, first aid and boats
  • Exposes problems (blocked routes, missing supplies)
