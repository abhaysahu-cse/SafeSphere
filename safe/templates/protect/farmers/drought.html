🌀 Cyclone — Farmers

Quick actions to reduce wind/surge damage — tie roofs, secure animals, early harvest.

Before

  • Anchor loose roof sheets
  • Move animals to sheltered areas
  • Harvest easily damaged crops early

During

  • Follow evacuation orders
  • Stay away from damaged trees or power lines

After

  • Check fields for saltwater intrusion
  • Contact extension services
