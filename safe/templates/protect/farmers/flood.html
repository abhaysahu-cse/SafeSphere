Frequently asked questions

How do I dry wet grains safely?
Spread grains thinly on raised tarpaulins in direct sun, turn frequently and use local extension advice for disinfection.
When should I harvest early?
If a red flood alert is issued and your crop is mature enough to be harvested economically, harvest early and store on raised platforms.

Quick actions

Use these when flood risk rises:

  • Move seed bags and valuables to top shelf or house roof (safe & dry).
  • Take animals to community high ground or boats if available.
  • Turn off irrigation pumps and electrical equipment.
