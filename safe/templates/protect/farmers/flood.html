Frequently asked

How do I dry wet grains?
Spread on clean tarpaulin, thin layer, direct sun, turn regularly. Discard badly moulded grains.
When to replant?
Test fields for salt and contamination; coordinate with KVK before large replanting.

Quick actions

  • Move seeds & grains to raised platform or top shelf
  • Move animals early to high ground
  • Secure pumps & small tools
  • Keep emergency bag: documents, medicines, torch
