Why cyclones cause large livelihood losses

🏚️

Roof & structure damage

High winds tear off roofs and destroy storage — simple strapping saves many roofs.

🌾

Crops lost to wind & rain

Standing crops and nurseries are vulnerable — early harvest or protection reduces loss.

🐄

Animals & equipment

Livestock exposed to wind or falling trees; pumps/tools washed or blown away.

🧭

Community disruption

Roads, power and communication fail — local coordination is essential.

Frequently asked

How to secure a tin roof quickly?
Tie sheets with ropes to rafters and anchor heavy objects on roofs; remove loose items that could be airborne.
Should I move my tractor before a cyclone?
If possible, move heavy machinery to sheltered sheds and anchor or wedge wheels. Otherwise immobilise and protect with coverings.

Quick actions

  • Secure loose sheets, tools and fuel drums.
  • Move animals to community shelter or higher ground.
  • Harvest or cover high-value crops if time permits.
  • Trim trees near houses and store important documents in waterproof bags.
