Why heatwaves threaten livelihoods

🔥

Crop heat stress

High temperatures reduce yield, cause flower drop and sunscald. Timely irrigation and shading help.

🐄

Livestock overheating

Animals suffer heat stress, reduce milk and growth; shade, water & cooling reduce losses.

💧

Water scarcity

Heat increases evaporation and competes for irrigation — plan water use carefully.

🪙

Income risk

Saving a small proportion of crop or a calf can preserve next season's income.

Frequently asked

How to protect livestock during a heatwave?
Provide shade, cool water frequently, use sprinklers/misting if possible, feed during cooler hours, check for signs of heat stress early.
Can I delay harvest when it’s very hot?
If crops are mature and heat risk threatens yield, consider early harvest and drying; consult local extension for market timing.

Quick actions

  • Store extra drinking water for people and animals
  • Provide shade (nets / tree shade) and cooling points for livestock
  • Work early or late — avoid midday field work
  • Mulch fields to preserve soil moisture
