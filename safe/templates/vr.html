How to use VR / 360° videos

Follow these quick tips for the best experience:

  • Use a headset: For full immersion, insert your phone into a cardboard-style headset (Google Cardboard or similar).
  • Orientation: Hold the device horizontally (landscape) before inserting into headset.
  • Controls: Tap the video, then press the headset’s magnet/click (if available) or use the phone screen to pause/play.
  • Fullscreen: Use the Fullscreen button below each video to expand before placing into a headset.
  • Audio: Use headphones for clearer sound and safety instructions.
  • Safe space: Stand still or sit on a swivel chair while viewing to avoid tripping.
  • Not 360? If the video is not encoded as 360, the headset will show it as a normal movie — still useful for training.

Tip: If a file doesn't play, check the static path or filename in your `static/video/` folder and update the template filenames accordingly.
