About this simulation

This AR experience demonstrates a flood simulation using WebXR. You can place a 3D house model on a horizontal surface detected by your device's AR capabilities. Once placed, you can start the flood simulation to see water levels rise around the house.

This simulation is intended for educational purposes to help users understand flood scenarios and safety measures.

Controls: Place & Start Flood. Use Stop to pause. Reload page to remove anchors.
